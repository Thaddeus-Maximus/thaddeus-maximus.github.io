Contact & About Thad

Monday, July 18, 2022

I’m an engineer from a small farm in Illinois. I grew up fixing equipment and getting my hands dirty. I went through 4-H and learned a slew of skills there, and started a bunch of robotics teams. I earned a BSME with minors in various computer fields at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology, while bring highly active in Formula SAE.

I am president of the Manchester Makerspace, run a blog on the intersection between technology and faith which has become a podcast, and engineer for DEKA R&D in New Hampshire.

My Resume.

I like to keep lots of irons in many fires. I’m involved with many things:

  • Mechanical Design
  • Fabrication
  • Simulation/Analysis of physical phenomena
  • All the scaffolding organization, infrastructure, and management to make that happen

If you want to reach me, shoot an email to thad@thadhughes.xyz. I love to answer questions about what I’ve done, or how I can help you.

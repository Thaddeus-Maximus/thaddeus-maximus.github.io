RoseLap v5

2018-07-03 12:55:00 +0300 » fsae, RoseGPE, Simulation, Software, Systems, Teams

Building on the success of RoseLap v3 (and the short-lived v4), I built the core for RoseLap v5. Check It Out!

Check out the whitepaper!

This is a lap-time simulation package; that is, it accepts simplified physical parameters of a car to simulate its performance around a racetrack. Combine this with a batcher and plotting tools, and it’s a fantastic suite for determining what type of car to build for the FSAE Competition.

Features include: - Four grip-ellipse tire model - Aerodynamic modes (DRS, normal, airbrake) - Engine curve definition - Support for CVT transmissions - Lateral Load Transfer Distribution - Stable “SS” Solver for line-and-arc type tracks - Finite shift times

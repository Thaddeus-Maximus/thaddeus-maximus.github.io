Bike Restoration

2019-08-14 16:21:00 » bikes, fabrication, odd

Found old bike for $20 - bought it to use for buoyant bike project. Not a terrible bike. Then I had my main nice bike stolen. So I figured I’d make this one a really nice looking one and buy an even worse bike for the buoyant bike project. Stripped everything down, powdercoated the frame, put it all back together. It’s not the best thing, but it’s a fun ride in the same way an FSAE car is fun - there’s nothing to it and it’s probably not very safe.
